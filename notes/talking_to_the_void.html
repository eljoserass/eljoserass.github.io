talking to the void

if you ask me the reason why, i would tell you it doesnt matter really

i have struggeled in the past with inaction due to doing too many passes over the question why, and not doing something unless the answer was clear.

sometimes things just have to be done because your body is asking you to.

curiosity, expression, testing your limits or just because are some good driving factors.

its a balance of course, doing everything impulsively is definetly not good.

how to now the difference has something to do with trusting your intuition, and having it to take peaces with your rationality.

as an example, the post itself is me maybe overthinking/overjustifying the reason of posting these while no one is reading, but doing it anyway because somehting inside me is making me do so.

it is soothing to accept that no one cares, no one will remember. i can simply let the thoughts flow, getting better one token at a time, getting used to sharing and documenting, on a platform I can delete-update anything I put out guilt-free

there is some aspect of ego and self pleasure to it. i have to recognize what I feel and think as valuable enough to do something just because I want and what I want is important.
